Night of the Ninja

Role & Ability Visual Guide

Ninjas

Spy

1-6

Look at another player's House card.

Mystic

1-6

Look at another player's House card and one of their Ninja cards.

Blind Assassin

1-6

Choose a player and Kill them.
(You cannot see their House card first.)

Shinobi

1-6

Look at another player's House card, then you may choose to Kill them.

Tricksters

Shapeshifter

1

Look at the House cards of two players. You may secretly swap them.

Grave Digger

2

Look at two discarded Ninja cards and pick one. You may play it immediately or keep it.

Troublemaker

3

Look at another player's House card. You may reveal it.

Spirit Merchant

4

Look at another player's Honor token or House card. You may swap one Honor token with them.

Thief

5

Reveal your House card. You may take one Honor token from a player who has more than you.

Judge

6

Reveal your House card. Choose a player and Kill them. (Mirror Monk and Martyr do not work.)

Special Cards

Mirror Monk

React

Reveal this if chosen to be Killed. Instead, you Kill them.

Martyr

React

Reveal this if chosen to be Killed. You are still Killed, but you gain an Honor token.

Mastermind

Special

If you are still alive during the Reveal phase, your House wins.
